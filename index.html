Guidelines

The first event of the Trihacker Tournament will be conducted by the Competitive Coding wing of GeekHaven . It will be held on 22nd September '15 and is open for all Btech first year students .

The event will be an onsite coding competition . The platform used will be Hackerearth.There will be only adhock problems. Information regarding time and venue will be given soon. For any queries, email us at technical.council@iiita.ac.in

Vision

The Technical Society wants to provide a platform to all the new members of IIITA family to show there skills and innovation in a very interesting series of events. GeekHaven proudly presents "Tri-Hacker" , first time in the short but glorious history of IIITA .

Why Trichaker Tournament ?

GeekHaven is proud to present the first version of Tri Hacker hacker tournament. Tri Hacker tournament would be a semester long tournament for freshers. Through out this tournament we aim to cultivate a community where students aren't afraid to push their boundaries and explore something new.
